Easy Bean Burrito Recipe

Description

A family favorite when you're short on time. Loved by kids and adults alike, bring a little fiesta into your lives.

In addition to being easy to make, these are also incredibly budget friendly!

Ingredients

  • 6" or 8" Tortillas
  • 3 cups Mexican Cheese
  • 1 can refried beans
  • Salsa and sour cream, if desired

Steps

  1. Warm the refried beans in a microwave for ~ 1 minute or until warm
  2. Spoon roughly a 1/4 cup of beans onto each tortilla (6 - 8 depending on size)
  3. Top each serving of beans with mexican cheese, roughly 2 - 3 pinches
  4. Wrap the tortilla up, folding in one end to secure beans and cheese
  5. Heat the tortilla over medium heat for 1 - 2 minutes per side, or until tortilla is lightly browned and cheese is melted
  6. Serve with salsa and sour cream
  7. Enjoy!
